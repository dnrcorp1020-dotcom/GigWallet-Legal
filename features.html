Income Tracking

All your platforms.
One unified dashboard.

GigWallet pulls earnings from 10+ gig platforms and consolidates them into a single, crystal-clear view — down to the individual trip level.

Direct Platform Sync

Powered by Argyle, GigWallet securely connects to Uber, DoorDash, Lyft, Instacart, and 6+ more platforms via read-only OAuth. Your data is yours — we never sell it.

  • Trip-level earnings with base pay, tips & bonuses
  • Per-trip mileage data imported automatically
  • Platform fees broken out separately
  • Deduplication prevents double-counting

Manual Income Entry

Cash tips, one-off gigs, or instant payments — log them in seconds with platform-specific categories, date tracking, and notes.

  • Quick-add for cash tips and instant payouts
  • Custom categories per platform
  • Multi-state income tracking for interstate workers
  • Date and notes for every entry

Income Intelligence

See gross vs. net earnings in real time — after platform fees are subtracted. Every dollar is automatically assigned to the right tax quarter.

  • Real-time gross vs. net calculations
  • Automatic tax year and quarter assignment
  • Platform performance comparison
  • Historical income pattern analysis
2
Expense & Receipt Tracking

Log expenses in seconds.
Never miss a deduction.

Three modes to capture every business expense — from a quick tap to a full receipt scan. Every dollar logged is a dollar saved at tax time.

Quick Add Mode

Pre-configured one-tap categories for the most common gig worker expenses. Minimal input, maximum speed.

  • Gas & fuel, phone bills, car maintenance
  • Tolls, parking, insurance, home office
  • Smart default deduction percentages
  • Category icons for instant recognition

Receipt OCR Scanner

Point your camera at any receipt. GigWallet's on-device OCR extracts the total, vendor, and date — then suggests the right expense category.

  • On-device processing — data never leaves your phone
  • Extracts amount, vendor name, and date
  • AI-powered category suggestion
  • Apply all fields or pick and choose

Full Manual Entry

Need full control? The detailed entry form lets you set every field — category, amount, vendor, date, deduction percentage, and an optional receipt photo.

  • Visual category grid selector
  • Custom tax deduction toggle (0–100%)
  • Backdate any expense
  • Attach receipt photo for records

Mileage Tracking

Log business miles and let GigWallet calculate your IRS deduction at the current standard rate. Platform trips from Uber and Lyft are imported for you.

  • IRS standard rate calculation built in
  • Trip mileage imported from connected platforms
  • Business vs. personal classification
  • Real-time deduction total

Privacy First — OCR Stays On-Device

Receipt scanning uses Apple's Vision framework on your device. Your receipts and financial data are never uploaded to a server for processing. Everything stays local via SwiftData.

Real-Time Tax Engine

Never get surprised
by a tax bill again.

GigWallet calculates your federal, state, and self-employment taxes in real time — every time you log income. Quarterly deadlines are always in view.

Quarterly Tax Countdown

Always know how many days until your next estimated payment is due — and exactly how much you owe. No more scrambling in April.

  • Days remaining until quarterly deadline
  • Estimated payment based on YTD income
  • Amount paid vs. amount owed tracker
  • Safe harbor compliance indicator

Tax Bite Visualization

See at a glance how much of today's earnings go to taxes — your effective rate combining federal, state, and self-employment obligations.

  • Effective tax rate (federal + state + SE tax)
  • YTD tax owed vs. paid comparison
  • Per-dollar tax impact on each shift
  • Tax reserve recommendations

Tax Vault

Set aside tax money as you earn it. The Tax Vault tracks your reserve balance and tells you if you're on track for each quarterly payment.

  • Manual deposits and withdrawals
  • Target balance tied to estimated liability
  • Year-over-year vault balance history
  • Synced with quarterly payment schedule

All 50 States Supported

GigWallet handles multi-state income with per-entry state tracking and state-specific tax rates. Work in Nevada and California? Both are covered.

No Surprises, Ever

Every dollar earned triggers an instant tax update. By the time a deadline arrives, you've been watching the number build all quarter long.
