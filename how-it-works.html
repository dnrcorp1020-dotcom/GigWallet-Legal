Platform Integration

How your gig accounts get linked.

GigWallet uses Argyle — an industry-standard employment data platform — to securely read your earnings data from gig platforms. Here's what that means in practice.

Read-Only Access

We can never touch your money

The connection is strictly read-only. GigWallet can see your earnings data — and nothing else. No ability to transfer funds, change settings, or take any action on your accounts.

Secure OAuth

You log in through the official platform

When linking an account, you authenticate directly with Uber, DoorDash, or whichever platform — the same login you use every day. GigWallet never sees your password.

  • OAuth industry standard authentication
  • End-to-end encryption in transit
  • You can revoke access any time
Trip-Level Data

Not just totals — every trip

Unlike apps that only see bank deposits, GigWallet imports individual trips with full breakdowns — so you know your profitability per trip, not just per week.

  • Base pay per trip
  • Tips, bonuses, platform fees
  • Miles driven per trip
  • Deduplication prevents double-counting

Manual Entry

Platform not listed? Add it manually.

For platforms not yet in the integration list — or cash tips and one-off gigs — manual income entry lets you log any amount with full categorization and date tracking.

Local Storage

Your data lives on your device

All your income, expense, and financial data is stored locally on your iPhone via SwiftData. It's private by default — not synced to external servers or shared with third parties.

Common Questions

Answers up front.

Do I need to link my gig accounts to use GigWallet?
No — platform linking is optional. You can use GigWallet entirely with manual income entry. Linking your accounts simply makes tracking faster and gives you trip-level detail without any data entry.
Is my login information safe when I link a platform?
Yes. You authenticate directly with each platform (Uber, DoorDash, etc.) using their own login flow. GigWallet and Argyle never see your username or password — only a secure read-only token is issued.
How does the tax calculator know my rate?
During setup, you provide your filing status and home state. GigWallet uses this along with your running YTD income to calculate your federal, state, and self-employment tax obligations in real time. All 50 states are supported.
What if I work in multiple states?
Each income entry can be assigned to a specific state. GigWallet applies the correct state tax rate per entry and calculates a blended state liability for your quarterly estimate.
When do the AI features become available?
Most AI features — like the Earnings Heatmap and Pattern Analysis — become more useful and accurate as you accumulate earnings data. The Work Advisor is available from day one and uses your location, gas prices, and local events even before you have history built up.
What's included in the free version vs. premium?
The free version includes full income tracking, expense logging (including receipt OCR), mileage tracking, the real-time tax engine, and basic dashboard analytics. Premium adds the Work Advisor AI, Financial Health Score, Earnings Heatmap, AI Intelligence Report, Financial Planner, and advanced analytics.
Is GigWallet available on Android?
GigWallet is currently available on iOS (iPhone). An Android version is on the roadmap — sign up for updates at hello@gigwallet.app.
