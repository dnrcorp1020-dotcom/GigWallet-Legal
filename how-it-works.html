Platform Integration

How your gig accounts get linked.

GigWallet uses Plaid — a trusted financial data platform — to securely connect to your bank and detect gig income deposits. Here's what that means in practice.

Read-Only Access

We can never touch your money

The connection is strictly read-only. GigWallet can see your earnings data — and nothing else. No ability to transfer funds, change settings, or take any action on your accounts.

Secure Bank Link

You log in through your own bank

When connecting, you authenticate directly with your bank through Plaid's secure Link flow. GigWallet never sees your bank username or password.

  • Bank-level encryption via Plaid
  • End-to-end encryption in transit
  • You can disconnect any time
Smart Matching

Auto-detects which platform paid you

GigWallet scans your bank deposits and automatically matches them to the right gig platform — Uber, DoorDash, Lyft, and more — so everything is categorized for you.

  • AI-powered transaction matching
  • Deposits sorted by platform
  • High-confidence matching (70%+ threshold)
  • Deduplication prevents double-counting
Supported Platforms

All the major platforms, one place

GigWallet detects income from 10+ gig platforms through Plaid bank sync.

  • Uber · Uber Eats
  • DoorDash · Instacart
  • Lyft · Grubhub
  • Amazon Flex · TaskRabbit
  • Upwork · Fiverr · and more
Manual Entry

Platform not listed? Add it manually.

For platforms not yet in the integration list — or cash tips and one-off gigs — manual income entry lets you log any amount with full categorization and date tracking.

Local Storage

Your data lives on your device

All your income, expense, and financial data is stored locally on your iPhone via SwiftData. It's private by default — not synced to external servers or shared with third parties.
